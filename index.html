Popular Cat Breeds

Persian

The Persian cat is known for its long, luxurious fur and calm demeanor.

Maine Coon

The Maine Coon is a large and friendly cat breed with a thick coat and tufted ears.

Sphynx

The Sphynx is a hairless breed known for its warm skin and playful personality.

Bengal

The Bengal cat is a highly active breed with distinctive leopard-like spots.

Siberian

Siberian cats are purebred cats with a formally registered ancestry

Charturex

The Chartreux is a rare breed of cat from France, and is recognised by a number of registries around the world.
